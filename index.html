I'm an Automation & Solutions Engineer with 12+ years of combined experience across technology and e-commerce operations. My work sits at the intersection of API integration, workflow engineering, and AI-driven automation — translating complex business requirements into reliable, scalable systems.

At MyOperator (VoiceTree Technologies), I design and deploy custom integration solutions for enterprise clients — connecting voice APIs, CRMs, and communication platforms through automation pipelines built in Workato, n8n, and Zapier. I've also developed AI-powered chatbot systems using ChatGPT and Gemini APIs, integrated into live support channels.

Before that, I spent three years managing multi-platform e-commerce operations across the UAE, maintaining around AED 100K in monthly revenue through a combination of cloud-hosted automation, inventory scripting, and data-driven marketing.

I work across the full stack of modern automation — from writing Python and Google Apps Script to building no-code portals in Bubble.io, to testing and validating APIs in Postman. I gather client requirements, write PRDs, and see integrations through from scoping to delivery.
